Cybersecurity layer

The command center surfaces security as a first-class story: pills, modal ribbon, and log lines reinforce E2E encryption, Zero Trust sessions, and auditability.

  • AES-256-GCM on emergency payloads; TLS 1.3 and rotating session keys in Virtual ER.
  • Zero Trust: JIT access, device attestation, mTLS service mesh, national key vault narrative.
  • Signed UAV downlink, encrypted wearable uplink, and hash-chain audit for SOS events.
